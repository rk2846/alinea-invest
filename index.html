About Alinea Invest

Alinea is new form of thought and we seek to inspire exactly that. We are dedicated to educating young women on the potential of cryptocurrencies and blockchain technology. Together - we want to foster a community and spark discussion to prepare and empower the future for all to be engaged and involved.

It’s crucial for the key players of the blockchain revolution to be like us: women. We believe in empowering all people to be at the forefront of evolving industries, from finance to agriculture to the arts. Since the world has historically been centralized & run mainly by men, in this decentralized revolution we need women to be at the forefront.

The world of blockchain sees growth, demand, and excitement, yet lacks in equal gender representation. Let’s unite & fix this issue.

About

The Problem

Women need to be at the forefront of revolution. Blockchain technology, a decentralized, transparent system based on the peer to peer network, is sweeping across all sectors from financial services to the arts and even agriculture. Currently, women represent only 9% of the entirety of the blockchain industry. This number is alarmingly low when considering blockchain’s potential to democratize individuals and disrupt industries. We, at Alinea Invest, are dedicated to empowering women to learn and engage with blockchain technology and cryptocurrencies. Through our website, events and various programs, we hope to foster a community, spark discussion, and empower the future of women.
